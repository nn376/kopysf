OUR MISSION

The King of Prussia Youth Sports Foundation is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization providing comprehensive development programs for boys and girls of all ages, races and creeds. We welcome every child who are happy and ready to have a fun time! Its primary goal is to offer a life-long progression of amateur sports programs that promote good sportsmanship, good citizenship and a sense of self-worth in students.Turning students and athletes into amazing athletes with their community of caring values from our community.

The Foundation’s programs in basketball, volleyball and track & field, provide a positive environment and a sound foundation for the physical, mental and moral development of student athletes. Though athletic development and competition is the centerpiece of our organization, our programs are designed to grow the whole person. To participate, you must be between 10 and 17 years of age. And because education is so important to your future, we ask that you maintain a B average in school.

Volunteering. No money, buy the rewards are many

As with any non-profit organization, it’s the people willing to volunteer their time and talents who will make this worthy effort work. We’re grateful to the many area residents who so graciously give off themselves. But of course, as our program grow, our needs grow.
We do hope you’ll join us as we help shape the futures of many area youth. And although we cannot compensate you for your time, clearly nothing is more rewarding than helping a young person blossom into a well-rounded citizen.
